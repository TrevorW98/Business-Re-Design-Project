Where it all started

In 1979, Diane Phillips opened D.A.D.’s Hot Dogs, originally named Dok Shoons at the time. Diane and her business partner Denise are the names and faces of D.A.D.’s (Diane and Denise) Hot Dogs. Celebrating now 40 years later, Diane proclaims that “to make a business work you need a great product, a great location, great customer service and great customers. I am happy to say we have all those things”. What separates D.A.D.’s Hot Dogs from others, are the quality ingredients that go into the hot dogs. These gourmet hot dogs are custom made from the bay area with half the fat and fewer preservatives than any other hot dog found at the grocery store. This is why Diane still eats her favorite chili dogs and takes some home to her 86-year-old father to this day.
